The Best Films According to Amanda

Films that Touched My Soul

*in no particular order*

  1. My Neighbor Totoro

    2. I love this Studio Ghibli film so much it's tatted on my body forever. Watching "My Neighborhood Totoro" feels like a warm hug from your loved ones after a long day.

  2. When Harry Met Sally

    3. This film was on repeat during one of the darkest periods of my life. My comfort movie when I need to remember that love is possible and it's probably right in front of you.

  3. Interstellar

    4. Heart breaking. Beautiful. A must watch. I was still crying well after it was over.
